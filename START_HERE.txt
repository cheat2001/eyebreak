Step 1: OPEN PROJECT
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
   $ open EyeBreak.xcodeproj
   
   OR double-click the .xcodeproj file in Finder


Step 2: CONFIGURE SIGNING
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
   1. Select "EyeBreak" target (blue icon)
   2. Click "Signing & Capabilities" tab
   3. Choose your Team from dropdown
      (Free Apple ID works fine!)


Step 3: BUILD & RUN
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
   Press ⌘R or click the ▶️ button
   
   Look for the eye icon in your menu bar!

After building and running:

1️⃣  GRANT PERMISSIONS
   ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
   ✓ Notifications (automatic request) → Click "Allow"
   ✓ Screen Recording (manual) → System Settings > Privacy & Security
   
2️⃣  START YOUR FIRST TIMER
   ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
   ✓ Click the eye icon in menu bar
   ✓ Press "Start Timer"
   ✓ Work for 20 minutes
   ✓ Take your first break!

3️⃣  CUSTOMIZE TO YOUR NEEDS
   ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
   ✓ Click eye icon > Settings
   ✓ Try Pomodoro mode (25/5)
   ✓ Adjust work/break intervals
   ✓ Choose your break style
